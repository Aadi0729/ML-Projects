About the Project

Mission

Welcome to the Student Performance Predictor application! This app is designed to help educators, parents, and students predict academic performance based on various parameters such as gender, race/ethnicity, parental level of education, lunch type, and test preparation course.

Goal

My goal is to provide insights into student performance, enabling informed decisions to enhance educational outcomes. By leveraging data science and machine learning techniques, I aim to identify patterns and correlations that can help improve student success.

About Me

This project was developed by Aditya Pateriya as part of an effort to bring data-driven decision-making to the educational sector. I believe that by understanding the factors influencing student performance, I can create a more effective and supportive learning environment.
